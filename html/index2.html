About Us

We are a group of enthusiastic individuals dedicated towards building a safe environment for upcoming pre-IB students. We’ve organized the LEAP (Leaders Engaged: Achieving Potential) conference for both mentors and delegates to build connections, share advice and learn. The theme for this year’s conference is space.

LEAP is September 12, 2017. Registration begins at 7:45 AM.
